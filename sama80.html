Samsung Galaxy A80

“SAMSUNG IS THE FIRST SMARTPHONE COMPANY TO COMBINE A SLIDER WITH A MOTORISED ROTATING CAMERA”

Let Me Introduce With Samsung Galaxy A80

Once again he proves he is the best out of every other smartphones .His innovation is at top level thanks “Samsung” for giving us such motorised hardware and infinity display we loved it and we sure user gonna also love it.

Samsung announces the Galaxy A80 is the first Samsung phone to feature a slider design, and it’s also the first phone to have a rotating motorised camera in a slider design. Well, let’s take a look at the new Galaxy A80.The Galaxy A80 has a glass and metal sandwich design.

Samsung Galaxy A80 features:
  • Look: Slider with a rotating triple camera
  • Camera: Main Camera 48MP(F2.0) || Ultra Wide camera of 8MP(F2.2) || ToF(Time-Of-Flight) with 3D deapth sensors.
  • Display: 6.7 Full HD+ Infinity Display (SuperAmoled)
  • Fingerprint: Under display fingerprint
  • Charger: Fast charging with 25w
  • Battery: 3700mAh Battery with fast C-Type charging
  • Audio Jack: No Audio Jack
  • Storage: 8GB RAM/128GB Internal Storage(no external SD card Support)
  • Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon 730(no confirmed yet)
  • Android: Pie 9.0 with Samsung ONE UI
  • Color: Gold | White | Black

"Galaxy A80 In Depth Information"

Display: Talking about the display, the Galaxy A80 features an Infinity Display 6.7inch FHD+ “1080×2400” Super AMOLED Display, which means it’s almost bezel-less, with no notch or punch-hole camera and also not having earpiece in display that’s cool isn’t it.

Camera: The fam of the Galaxy A80 is it’s camera setup, it’s new rotating motorised cameras take eye of the audience towards it. The phone has a triple lens camera setup, which includes a 48MP f/2.0 primary camera, an 8MP ultra wide angle camera and a 3D depth (ToF)camera. Now, this camera setup can be used to take some nice selfies and portrait selfies.When we Switch to the selfie mode in the Galaxy A80, the three cameras automatically pop-up from the back of the phone and flip. It looks aswm, and it will take you 48MP detailed selfies, ultra wide angle selfies and great portrait mode selfies and that you gonna love it.

Battery: If we talk about battery it come up with 3700mAh battery(non removable) with Type -C fast charging of 25W.

Processor: On board it having ” Qualcomm Snapdragon 730G Processor” which is superfast and perform multiple processing simultaneously which means great gaming experience.

Fingerprint: As we see that it come up with under screen fingerprint scanner which means phone unlock quickly. That we see now a days in every flagships smartphones, that pretty aswm.

Galaxy A80 Price and Availability

The Galaxy A80 will be available in 3 color variants: Angel Gold, Ghost White and Phantom Black and while Samsung hasn’t revealed the price of the phone yet. It’s said to go on sale from May 29.
