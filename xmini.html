Let Me Introduce With Pocket Nano-X Wireless Speaker

By: Kaushal Kishor | Posted:22May2019

In this modern era music has been the relaxing pill for everyone, We all are likes to move with music, So bluetooth speakers plays a very fabulous role, It makes the music environment everywhere on the go. So in this trend of blutooth speakers X-mini Company had launched Nano-X which is light and portable.

The NANO-X boasts high quality drivers that create exquisite sound in a compact and fashionable package. Constructed with a sleek metal body, the highly durable X-mini NANO-X comes with several features to produce a truly immersive audio experience from a compact Bluetooth speaker. It is also wireless enabled thanks to integrated Bluetooth technology, the NANO-X requires no frustrating wires for music playback so you can listen to your favourite tunes wirelessly for up to 6 hours.

Color Variants: Grey | Blue | Red

Nano-X features:
  • Dimensions: 36.8mm x 43mm
  • Speaker Magnetically shielded 33mm
  • Loudness Output 2W
  • Frequency response: 20Hz - 20kHz
  • Signal-to-noise: 90dB
  • Distortion:1.0 %
  • Full Range:33mm
  • Battery: 300mAh Lithium ion batteries
  • Battery Charge Time: Max 2hr.
  • Playback Time: Upto 6hr.
  • Weight:39.9g
  • Audio Jack: No Audio Jack
  • Mic: Yes, mic Available
  • Color: Grey | Red | Blue

"Nano-X mini In Depth Information"

  • The X-mini NANO-X brings great sound quality in a small factor. Slip it in your bag on your commute to work or gym for when you work out. Your music goes whenever you need it to. Enjoy hands-free calls with a built-in microphone in the X-mini NANO-X. The X-mini NANO-X is designed for you, not only to enjoy your favourite tracks but also capture all the fun moments.

  • This special feature means you can place your Bluetooth device to one side and use the remote, rather than your media device itself, to capture a moment remotely. A meticulous design has produced an astoundingly compact speaker that produces crisp audio quality, normally produced by far larger speakers. Conveniently carry it with you, wherever you are heading, so your favourite music can light up your day anytime, anywhere.
